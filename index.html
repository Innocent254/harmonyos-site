What's New

Every part rebuilt.
Nothing borrowed.

HarmonyOS 6 drops Android entirely — no AOSP, no Linux kernel. It runs on Huawei's own HongMeng microkernel with a fully native stack from chip to UI.

🤖

XiaoYi AI 6

Your personal AI partner. Executes complex multi-step tasks, recognises dialects, reads medical reports, and offers "Sees the World" mode for visually impaired users — all on-device.

🛡️

StarShield Security

Kernel-to-cloud AI security. Real-time scam-call detection, deepfake protection, automatic "anti-peep" privacy screen when the camera spots a second viewer.

📡

Cross-Device Super

Phone, tablet, PC, watch, TV, earbuds — all one device. Share files with Apple devices too. Drag files between screens. One clipboard, everywhere.

✨

Harmonious Aesthetics

Glassmorphism meets spatial light. Vivid system colours, reflective glow, blur overlays, and context-aware animations that respond to what you're doing.

📸

AIGC Camera

Scene-aware portrait retouching, one-tap light & depth adjustment, AI-one-click filming from stills to animated clips, and AIGC wallpaper generation from your own handwriting.

⚡

HongMeng Microkernel

Built from scratch for efficiency. Faster context-switching, lower interrupt latency, driver containers for hardware compatibility — and no Android Runtime overhead.

💊

Live Capsules

Like Dynamic Island but native and system-wide. Any app can show a "capsule" — a persistent, live widget on the lock screen or status bar — for sports scores, delivery tracking, music, and more.

🏥

AI Health Assistant

Voice consultations, insurance code access, hospital navigation, and medical report interpretation via iFLYTEK integration — right from the system UI without opening an app.

🌐

ArkUI + ArkTS

A modern, declarative UI framework (like SwiftUI or Jetpack Compose) for building apps that run beautifully on phones, tablets, wearables and TVs — all from one codebase.

XiaoYi AI 6

Your smart partner,
not just an assistant.

XiaoYi goes beyond voice commands. It understands context across apps, organises research, detects scams mid-call, and helps visually impaired users "see" the world through the camera — all running on-device for privacy.

  • ◆
    Multi-step autonomous tasks

    Tell XiaoYi to "book a table for two near my 3 PM meeting" — it reads your calendar, finds restaurants, checks availability, and confirms without you lifting a finger.

  • ◆
    Sees the World mode

    Point the camera at anything — a street sign, a document, a product label — and XiaoYi describes it in real time. Designed for accessibility, useful for everyone.

  • ◆
    Dialect-aware voice recognition

    HarmonyOS 6's voice engine understands major Chinese dialects and multiple languages, making it usable for far broader populations than competitors.

Design Language

Harmonious Aesthetics —
light, blur, and vivid colour.

Chief UX Designer Yang Zhiyan's design philosophy: an OS that feels alive. Inspired by the physics of light — reflective, glowing, translucent — sitting between skeuomorphism and flat design.

Cross-Device

One OS.
Every screen.

HarmonyOS 6 powers everything from 6-inch phones to 85-inch TVs, smartwatches, PCs, and even car dashboards — all on the same native kernel.

📱
Smartphones
Mate 80 series, Pura 80, Nova 14, and all flagship lines. HarmonyOS 6 ships pre-installed on all new Huawei phones in China.
📟
Tablets
MatePad Pro series with desktop-class multi-window, stylus support, and seamless phone handoff for every open app.
⌚
Smartwatches
Huawei Watch 5 and GT series. Health sensors feed AI Health Assistant directly. Glance notifications via capsule on the watch face.
💻
PCs (HarmonyOS Computer)
Following Huawei's Windows licence expiry in March 2025, the MateBook Pro now runs HarmonyOS 5/6 natively on a full desktop UI.
📺
Smart TVs
Huawei Vision TV series. Phone-to-TV projection, voice control via XiaoYi, and shared clipboard across all devices on the same network.
🎧
Audio / Earbuds
FreeBuds and SoundJoy series integrate with the system audio stack, with AI noise reduction that adapts to your environment automatically.
🚗
Smart Cars
HarmonyOS powers Huawei-partnered vehicles' cockpits. Phone and car share contacts, navigation, and media with zero latency handoff.
🏠
Smart Home / IoT
Tens of thousands of HiLink / Matter-compatible devices. Control them all from the Super Device panel — no third-party app needed.
